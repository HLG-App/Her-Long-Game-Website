A note on financial content

Her Long Game provides financial education. The information we share is general in nature. It is designed to help you build knowledge, confidence and understanding around money, financial systems and long-term decision-making.

Her Long Game does not provide personal financial advice. Nothing on our website, in our emails, products, resources, tools, app or content takes into account your personal objectives, financial situation or individual needs.

Before making financial decisions, you should consider your own circumstances and seek advice from a qualified professional where appropriate.

What personal information we collect

The personal information we collect depends on how you interact with Her Long Game. We may collect:

  • your name
  • your email address
  • your phone number, if you choose to provide it
  • information you provide when joining our waitlist or signing up to updates
  • information you provide when downloading a resource or purchasing a product
  • survey responses, feedback or messages you choose to send us
  • website usage information, such as pages visited, links clicked and general analytics data
  • technical information, such as browser type, device information, IP address and general location data
  • app account and usage information, as described in the Her Long Game app section below

We only collect information that is reasonably necessary for our business activities.

How we collect your information

We collect personal information when you:

  • visit our website
  • join our waitlist

  • download a free resource
  • purchase a product or service
  • complete a form or survey
  • contact us by email, website, social media or another channel

Why we collect and use your information

We collect and use your personal information to:

  • provide Her Long Game products, resources and services

  • manage business operations, legal records, accounting and tax obligations

  • comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

Email marketing

If you join our waitlist, download a resource, purchase from us or sign up to receive updates, we may send you emails about Her Long Game, including education, product updates, offers and launch information.

You can unsubscribe at any time by using the unsubscribe link in our emails or contacting us at hello@herlonggame.com.au.

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to help our website work properly, understand how people use our website and improve Her Long Game.

Cookies may collect information such as your browser type, device information, IP address, pages visited, links clicked and time spent on the website. You can usually disable cookies through your browser settings. Some parts of the website may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

Where this information identifies you, or could reasonably identify you, we treat it as personal information.

Payment information

If you purchase a product or service from Her Long Game, payment information may be collected and processed by a third-party payment provider. We do not store your full payment card details. Payment providers handle payment information in accordance with their own privacy and security practices.

Her Long Game app

When you use the Her Long Game app, we collect and store additional personal information to provide the app experience. All app data is stored on servers located in Australia (Sydney).

Account information

To use the app, you create an account. We collect and store your display name, email address and a secured, encrypted password credential. We also record when your account was created and when you last signed in.

We use this information to create and manage your account, authenticate your access, personalise your experience and communicate with you about your account.

Emotional baseline and current state

As part of your onboarding and ongoing experience, the app may ask you to describe your current emotional relationship with money. We store an emotional baseline recorded when you join, and an emotional current state updated as you progress.

We use this information to personalise your experience, track your progress and, in de-identified form, to understand how Her Long Game affects the women who use it over time. This information is sensitive. We do not share it with third parties in identifiable form.

Financial Wellbeing Diagnostic

The app includes a Financial Wellbeing Diagnostic, which you complete during onboarding and may repeat as you progress. Your responses are used to calculate a score and assign an archetype that reflects your current financial starting point.

We store your diagnostic score, archetype and completion status against your account. We do not store your individual question responses against your account.

We may collect anonymised, de-identified diagnostic data for research and product improvement. This data cannot be linked back to you as an individual and is not personal information.

Lesson progress and in-lesson responses

We store your progress through Her Long Game lessons and modules, including which lessons you have started and completed, your current position within lessons and the timestamps of your activity.

Some lessons invite you to reflect and write a personal response as part of the learning experience. Where you choose to write a response, we store that response against your account and progress record. These responses are personal reflections you have chosen to record. They are not shared with other users or third parties in identifiable form.

We use lesson progress and response data to personalise your experience, enable you to resume where you left off and improve our content over time.

Her Notes

The app includes a Her Notes feature where you can record personal reflections prompted by lesson content. Where you choose to write a note, we store the prompt, your response and the date it was created against your account.

Her Notes are personal. They are not shared with other users or third parties in identifiable form. You can request deletion of your Her Notes at any time by contacting us at hello@herlonggame.com.au.

Financial tools and calculators

The app includes financial education tools and calculators. Where you use a tool, we store your inputs and the calculated result against your account so you can return to and review your previous work.

Tool inputs and outputs are stored for your personal use only. They are not shared with other users or third parties in identifiable form. These tools are provided for educational purposes only and do not constitute personal financial advice.

Goals

The app allows you to set and track financial goals. Where you create a goal, we store the goal label, type, target date, target amount and progress status against your account. Goal data is stored for your personal use only and is not shared with other users or third parties in identifiable form.

Bookmarks

You may bookmark lessons, tools or content within the app. We store your bookmarks and any associated notes against your account so you can return to content you have saved.
